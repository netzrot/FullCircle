FullStory put the news in perspective.

During the 2016 presidential election, there was a lot of talk about the political bubbles we all find ourselves in. This website is an attempt to burst that bubble. I wanted to create a simple tool to show the way in which liberal and conservative news cover the same topics differently, how they stress certain aspects and ignore others based on their political affilication. I also wanted to show which stories get the most attention on the respective websites.

For this early prototype, I based my selection of left and right news sources on AllSideâ€™s bias rating. The Associated Press was included as a centrists news source.

I hope this website helps readers navigate the news and be more aware of the media bias. I also hope that journalists find it useful in their work and to be reminded of their own biases.
